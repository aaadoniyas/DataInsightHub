About Anthropometric Data

Anthropometric measurements are used in ergonomics, product design, architecture, and clothing industries to create products that properly fit the human body and enhance comfort and safety.

Gender-Specific Analysis

Male and female bodies have different proportions. Our tool provides separate analysis for each gender, allowing for more precise design considerations in gender-specific products and applications.
